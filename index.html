Hi, I’m Jenni

A user experience designer from NYC

I most recently worked with the creative folks at Paperless Post as a Product Designer, with a focus on user research. Before this, I collaborated with Beyond agency on UX strategy for a mobile healthcare app; taught + developed UX curriculum for General Assembly; and edited a book on experience design "Design for Dasein."

I design for mobile + desktop, and have an inclusive process with PMs + devs. I believe design thinking is most impactful when supported by data + real user feedback.

My Toolkit

Design

Aside from doodling by hand, Sketch is my tool of choice for wireframes + designs. I use OmniGraffle for quick diagramming.

Organize

Trello, Notion, and ToDoist help manage my workflow. In teams, I've used Pivotal Tracker and JIRA within the scrum framework.

Prototype

InVision App is my go-to for quick multi-screen prototypes, while I use coffeescript in Framer for more transition-heavy designs.

Research

I use SurveyMonkey and Qualtrics for surveys and results analysis, but I'm always on the lookout for new research tools!

Annotate

Zeplin annotations have been well received in my experience, but I'm open to the method preferred by my dev team!

Test

Validately helps me conduct moderated/unmoderated tests remotely. Inspectlet is useful for observing live sessions.
